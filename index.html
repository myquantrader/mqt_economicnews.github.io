How It Works

2

Filter & Rank Events

3

Trigger Alerts

Audio alerts notify you ahead of scheduled releases.

4

FAQ

Does this repaint?

No. News events are timestamp‑based and static.

Can I move the panel?

Yes. Use preset locations or drag it anywhere on the chart.

Does it work on any market?

Yes. Futures, crypto, forex, and equities are all supported.

Is an internet connection required?

Yes. News is downloaded periodically from the source feed.
